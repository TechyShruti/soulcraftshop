    Gift shop product design involves selecting, creating, and curating a range of items that are suitable for gifting. This includes considering the target audience, the types of occasions the gifts will be for, and the overall aesthetic of the shop. It also involves display and presentation, as well as understanding popular and seasonal items.

    Identify the Niche and Target Audience

    Here's a more detailed look.
  • Specialization: Consider focusing on a specific niche like handmade items, local crafts, or items related to a particular interest e.g. diary.
  • Demographics: Think about who will be buying the gifts. Are They for children, adults, tourists, or a mix.
  • Occasions: Identify common gift-giving occasions(birthdays,holidays, weddings, etc.) and tailor products accordingly
